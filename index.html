About Rajkiya Polytechnic Sahajanwa

Established in 1997, Rajkiya Polytechnic Sahajanwa is a premier technical institute in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, offering diploma courses in various engineering disciplines.

Our institute is affiliated with the Board of Technical Education, Uttar Pradesh, and approved by AICTE. We are committed to providing quality technical education to create skilled professionals for the industry.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry-relevant curriculum, we strive to nurture innovation and excellence among our students.
